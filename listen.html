Sign language sample

A list of sign languages to be recognized for each genre.
Please see this and use it for studying sign language.

Greetings1

Good morning
Make your right hand goo and bring it to the side of your hand. Just lower the fist down. Stand the index fingers of both hands facing each other. Bend your index finger.

Hello
Align your index and middle fingers and bring them to the center of your forehand. Stand the index fingers of both hands facing each other. Raise your index finger.

Good evening
Banzai with both hand in front of the palm. Cross your hands in front of your fase. Put the index fingers of both hands facing each other. Bend your index finger.

Good night
Put your hands together next to your fase and make a sleeping pose.

Thank you
Bring your extended finger in front of your fase and bow.

Greetings２

Good bye
Raise your hand to the left end right. repeat

Thank you
Plase your light hand on the left end right insteps with your left palm fasing down. Raise your lighthand.At this time,bow your head.

Sorry
Bring your index finger and the hand pinched in front of your fase in front of your fase. Strech your fingers and lower your head whire lowering your hands.

reply

I see
Plase your hand in front of your chest. Lower your hand as it is.

No
Webe your hand frome side to side.Neck also shakes. Repeat.

I don't know
Cross your hand in front of your fase.

I can do it
Raise the thumb of your right hand and attach it to the fold. Move your hand to the right and put it on your light shouder.

It's different
Extend the thumb and index finger of both hands and face each other.

feeling

I'm happy
Bend the fingers of both hands toword you. Alternately lower both hands.

Do one's best
Bring your hands to the front of your chest.Stretch your knees. Lower both hands twice to the stomach

I'm angry
Bend the fingers of both hands in front of the tummy. Raise both hands to the front of your chest.

I was surprised
bend the index and middle fingers of the right hand onto the left hand. Raise your right hand up.

Excuse me
Bring your hand pinched with your index finger and thumb in front of your face. Stretch your fingers and lower your hands while lowering your head.

school

Lunch
Align your index and middle fingers and bring them to the center of your forehead. The left hand is a gesture with a bowl, and the middle finger and index finger of the right hand are extended to the mouth. Lunch is the same sign language.

Homework
Put the fingertips of both hands in front of your chest. (House shape) With the left hand as it is, move to write with the right hand.

English
Align the index and middle fingers of your right hand next to your left cheek. Trace to the right cheek as it is.

Library
Open both hands together. (Book shape) Place the palms of both hands toward you in front of your chest. Open both hands to shoulder width and pose in front of you.

School
Turn the palms of both hands toward you. Raise and lower your hands twice up and down.
