How to use recording

This feature allows people who are unable to speak in public to record their own voice so that they can speak.
They record the audio at home and play it back at school for use.

1.At first

When the page opens, it will ask you if you want to use the microphone, select the "right button".

2.How to record

class="setumei"Press the red "Record" button on the left to record the text or greeting you want to use.
When you have finished recording, press the "stop" button on the right to stop recording.
Please enter the recorded text or greeting

3.How to play

Press the triangle button above the sentence you want to play to play back the recorded sentence or greeting.
You can also use the "Delete" button at the bottom right to erase the recorded text.
